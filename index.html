Customer Service- Making the First Move

It is not just about greeting customer when they seek service but it is more about HOW we greet them? Are we greeting the customer robotically or with enthusiasm? Are these customers walking away thinking that we were just doing our job or are they thinking that we genuinely cared? Demonstrating the first move in customer service is crucial in that it

  • Creates a positive first impression
  • Encourages engagement
  • Builds trust and loyalty
  • Improves efficiency
